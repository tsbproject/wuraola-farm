Welcome to Wuraola Royal Farm and Agro Allied Limited

Leading producer of palm oil, palm kernel oil, and sustainable animal husbandry in Osun State, Nigeria.

.

Company Profile

Wuraola Royal Farm & Agro Ltd is a dynamic agricultural enterprise based in the serene community of Iregun Ijesha, Osun State, Nigeria. We are deeply rooted in sustainable agriculture and committed to producing high-quality farm outputs with a strong focus on palm tree farming, processing of palm oil and palm kernel oil, and diverse animal husbandry operations.

We pride ourselves on a fully integrated value chain — from cultivation and harvesting to processing and packaging — ensuring that every product from our farm meets the highest standards of quality, affordability, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To become the leading palm oil and palm kernel oil business in Nigeria within the next ten years.

Our Mission

  • To farm, process, and sell high-quality, conveniently packaged, and affordable palm oil, palm kernel oil, and by-products in compliance with international standards.
  • To ensure sustainable utilization of land and labor while adopting advanced production technologies for higher yield, profitability, and resilience to environmental stress.
  • To continuously explore and implement modern processing methods that guarantee maximum oil extraction and minimal waste.

Corporate Objectives

  • To provide palm products that meet the expectations of both households and industries.
  • To ensure that every home in Iregun-Ijesha and Osogbo enjoys healthy and attractive gallons of palm oil and kernel oil within the first five years of full operation.
  • To establish a well-equipped processing facility that ensures high efficiency, productivity, and reduced processing losses.

Long-Term Goals

  • To utilize all palm by-products effectively for cost reduction and added revenue.
  • To expand oil palm cultivation from 10 acres to 30 acres, supporting scalability and improved yield.
  • To break into international markets through the exportation of palm oil and palm kernel oil.

Our Core Values

  • Consumer Satisfaction – We put our customers first by delivering only the best quality products.
  • Environmental Sustainability – Every farming and processing decision is made with care for the ecosystem.
  • Wuraola Royal Farm & Agro Ltd is not just a farm — it’s a vision in motion. With the backing of a dedicated team, fertile land, and a forward-thinking approach, we’re building a legacy of agricultural excellence, community development, and environmental stewardship in Osun State and beyond.

Wuraola Royal Farm & Agro Ltd is committed to sustainable palm oil, kernel oil production and animal husbandry in Iregun-Ijesha, Osun State.
